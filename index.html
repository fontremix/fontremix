Quick Guide

  1. Choose a category or pick a font from the list
  2. Type your preview text
  3. Adjust sliders and toggles
  4. Enter a file name
  5. Click "Download Font"

Why some features are limited

  • Extreme transforms can distort outlines or spacing
  • Monospace forces equal widths (can change rhythm)
  • Flip/invert can stress hinting in some families
  • Height extends beyond ttf boundaries at certain values
  • Limits keep TTFs reliable across apps

Why these fonts are free

Google Fonts hosts open-licensed families (often SIL Open Font License or Apache 2.0). These allow personal/commercial use and modification. These fonts and any modifications you make to them are 100% free-to-use :)
